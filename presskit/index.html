Description

Something went wrong. While you were exploring uncharted space an unknown wormhole took you off course. What you do know is that your warp engine aren't powerful enough to open it again. Use what you have, look for ressources and try to find a way back to civilization.

  • Transform items into energy to survive
  • Fight enemies
  • Use your energy to craft new weapons and ships
  • Go through 12 increasingly difficult solar systems until your reach the ultimate artifact
  • Run back up with the artifact to complete the game and record your high-score
  • Now do it again and try to beat your best score!

The game is updated regularly to add more content and fix any issues. It already features :

  • 18 unique ships
  • hundreds of weapons
  • scanners, utilities, shields, converter and dozens of other items to customize your ship and create unique synergies
  • Multiple celestial object like planets, suns, anomalies and more!
  • 12 main levels and 3 optional branches
  • 3 races each with unique characteristics and story
  • Unique challenges

History

I'm a single, independent developper. I've worked many years in the game industry before leaving my current job to start my own independent studio to make the games I like. Solar Rogue is based off an old ASCII based space roguelike prototype I made years ago updated for todays graphics while also trying to keep the traditional roguelike look. It's being developped using the Open Source Godot Game Engine. I also have a youtube channel where I post developpement progress for the game and various deep dive into the programming challenges of game making.
